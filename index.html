About the Lab

The Mahindra University 6G Innovation Lab is dedicated to advancing next-generation wireless technologies through research, experimentation, and real-world testbeds. The lab focuses on 5G/6G system design, AI-enabled wireless networks, edge computing, and secure communication architectures, bridging academia and industry.
