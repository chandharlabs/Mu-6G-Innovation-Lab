Faculty Lead

Dr. Subbarao Boddu

Associate Professor

6G Innovation Lab
Mahindra University

Dr. Subbarao Boddu is the Faculty Lead of the 6G Innovation Lab at Mahindra University. He completed his Ph.D. in Wireless Communications from Indian Institute of technology, Kharagpur in 2014. He joined Mahindra University in 2017 and has since been actively involved in teaching, research, and building next-generation wireless systems. Click here for more details.

  • Research Areas: 5G/6G, Internet of Things, AI for Wireless, Network Security
